Introduing The Arduino

The Arduino is a microprocessor which is equipped with the chip ATmega 328. The Arduino can take analog or digital signals from sensors and give out digital or PWM output. The Arduino is great for prototyping and now comes in many variants including much smaller ones such as the Arduino Pro mini which is just as powerful as the Arduino Uno and has the same amount of pins.It can be used for many simple or complex projects. It can be used for many projects such as a small home automation project right to a remote control car.
